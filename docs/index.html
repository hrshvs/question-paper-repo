About This Archive

This archive contains course materials from various departments at IISER Mohali:

  • Biology - BIO courses covering molecular biology, genetics, and more
  • Chemistry - CHM courses including organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry
  • HSS - Humanities and Social Sciences courses
  • IDC - Interdisciplinary courses covering various topics
  • Math - MTH courses from calculus to advanced mathematics
  • Physics - PHY courses covering classical to quantum physics

Quality varies; some scans are rough. This is a student-run archive intended for learning and exam prep.

Maintained by the IISER Mohali student community
